# Economy

## Credits

Credits are the main currency. You earn them by selling ore and spend them on supplies, payroll, and market purchases.

New accounts receive a signup credit bonus (see server `credits.json` for current values).

## Finances panel

Open **Finances** to review:

- Current balance
- Recent transactions
- Runway estimate (how many days you can operate at current burn)
- Emergency ore buyback

## Trade Market vs Store

| Panel | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Trade Market** | Buy supplies and sell ore at market-linked or NPC prices |
| **Store** | Buy supplies for your mine |

## Game day

The game runs on **UTC days**. Market prices and daily simulation refresh at UTC midnight on live servers.

Advancing the day processes mining output, payroll, and supply usage for your active mine.

## Trade items

Ore and supply types available on the trade market are configured on the server. Prices and catalog data may change between releases — check the in-game market for current values.
