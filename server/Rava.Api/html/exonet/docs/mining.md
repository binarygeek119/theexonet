# Mining

## Mine grid

Your asteroid mine is an **8×8 grid** of zones. Click a zone to inspect ore, depth, and assigned workers.

## Workers

- Assign workers to zones from the zone detail panel.
- Workers extract ore over time when the game day advances.
- Payroll and supply consumption are calculated on each day tick.

## Ore and shipping

- Mined ore can be sold through the **Trade Market** (NPC refinery).
- Use **Shipping** to load ore into your cargo hold before selling.
- **Emergency Ore Buyback** (50% value) is available from Finances if you need credits to avoid a soft lock.

## Supplies

Supplies power mining operations. Track consumption in **Finances** and restock from the **Store** or **Trade Market**.

Supply prices can follow live US market data when enabled on the server.
